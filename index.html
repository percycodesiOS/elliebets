The Edge

Three signals the books move on slowly.

Sportsbook traders post a line and let it sit. You watch the conditions change in real time. Here's the edge surface EllieBets puts in front of you.

💧

Dew point + real-feel delta

When dew is above 65°F and real-feel is 6°+ above actual temp, the air is thick. Ball flight suppressed. Scoring conditions deteriorate. The tool flags it before the books reprice round totals.

🌬️

Live wind from Windfinder

Sustained wind ≥12 mph from a known direction reshapes who can score. Ball-strikers gain, putters lose. Wind direction + gusts shown on a compass tied directly to the event.

⛅

Apple Weather precip + temp

Suspension risk lives in the precip number. Real-feel lives in the temp number. Both pull from the same source you're already checking — but right next to your bet slip.
